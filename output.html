Output Indicators Trend Analysis Report (2021–2024)

Overview

This retrospective analysis examines Sierra Leone's malaria program performance from 2021 to 2024, assessing the extent to which planned targets were met across 15 output indicators spanning case testing, treatment, facility reporting, community care, and vector control. The analysis is intended to identify gaps, highlight trends, and provide an evidence base for programmatic decision-making and future planning by the National Malaria Control Programme (NMCP).
